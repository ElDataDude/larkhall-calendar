How to Subscribe

Google Calendar

  1. Open Google Calendar
  2. Click the "+" next to "Other calendars"
  3. Select "From URL"
  4. Enter the calendar URL shown above
  5. Click "Add calendar"

Apple Calendar (macOS)

  1. Open the Calendar app
  2. Select File > New Calendar Subscription
  3. Enter the calendar URL shown above
  4. Click "Subscribe"
  5. Configure options (auto-refresh, alerts, etc.)
  6. Click "OK"

Apple Calendar (iOS)

  1. Go to Settings > Calendar > Accounts
  2. Tap "Add Account"
  3. Select "Other"
  4. Tap "Add Subscribed Calendar"
  5. Enter the calendar URL shown above
  6. Tap "Next" and then "Save"

Microsoft Outlook

  1. Open Outlook and go to Calendar view
  2. Select "Add Calendar" > "From Internet"
  3. Enter the calendar URL shown above
  4. Click "OK"
  5. Name the calendar and click "OK"

FAQ

How often is the calendar updated?

The calendar is updated daily at 4:00 AM UTC to reflect the latest fixture information.

Will I be notified of fixture changes?

Your calendar application will automatically sync with any changes to the fixtures. Notification settings depend on your calendar application's configuration.

Can I add this calendar to multiple devices?

Yes, you can subscribe to the calendar on as many devices as you wish.

Is there a mobile app?

No dedicated app is needed. Simply subscribe to the calendar in your preferred calendar application on your mobile device.
